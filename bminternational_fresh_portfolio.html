About Company

Your Trusted Partner in Pakistan

BM International (BMI) is a premier textile sourcing, export management, and buying house based in Karachi, Pakistan. We bridge global fashion brands, retail chains, and wholesale importers with compliance-audited manufacturing plants across Pakistan.

Factory Sourcing & Audit

Strict vetting of WRAP, BSCI, and ISO certified factories tailored to your product line.

AQL Quality Assurance

Inline, pre-shipment, and final AQL 2.5 quality control inspections by on-ground engineers.

Price Negotiation

Direct yarn-to-garment cost breakdown to secure competitive volume pricing.

Export Logistics

Complete customs clearance, shipping documentation, and container loading supervision.
