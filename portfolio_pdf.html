About BM International

Your Sourcing Partner in Pakistan

BM International (BMI) is a Pakistan-based garments and textile sourcing, export, and buying agent company. We manage the entire supply chain — from factory vetting, price negotiation, and fabric sampling to AQL quality control inspections and container shipping — delivering finished goods directly to international brands.

Factory Sourcing & Audit

We vet compliance-certified factories across Pakistan to match exact product specs.

End-to-End Quality Inspection

Multi-stage inline and final AQL 2.5 quality control before any shipment leaves Pakistan.

Competitive Price Negotiation

Leveraging deep local industry relationships to secure the best mill prices for global buyers.

Logistics & Export Management

Door-to-door or door-to-port international freight and customs clearance management.
