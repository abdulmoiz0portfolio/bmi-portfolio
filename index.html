About BMI

Your Trusted Buying Agent in Pakistan

BM International (BMI) is a Pakistan-based garments and textile sourcing, export, and buying agent company. We do not give buyers direct access to factories — instead, BMI acts as the intermediary who does all the work: sourcing, negotiating, managing production, quality inspection, and delivering finished results.

Our team has deep expertise in the Pakistani textile industry, enabling us to find the best factories, negotiate competitive prices, and ensure quality standards that meet international expectations.

What We Do

Our Services

From initial sourcing to final delivery, BMI manages the entire supply chain so you can focus on growing your brand.

Sourcing & Procurement

We identify and vet the best factories across Pakistan for your specific product needs — cotton, denim, fleece, knits, and woven garments.

Quality Inspection

Our team conducts multi-stage quality inspections — from raw material checks to final AQL inspections — ensuring every shipment meets your specifications.

Production Management

We oversee the entire manufacturing process — timelines, sampling, bulk production — to ensure your order stays on track and on budget.

Negotiation & Pricing

Leveraging our network and industry knowledge, we negotiate the most competitive prices without compromising on quality.

Logistics & Shipping

Door-to-port or door-to-door — we coordinate with freight forwarders and customs to ensure smooth, on-time international delivery.

Custom Labeling & Packaging

Private labeling, branded packaging, hang tags, and care labels — we handle all branding requirements to your exact specifications.
